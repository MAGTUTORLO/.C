how to fix USB ports not Detecting:

1. click "START "menu

2. searh "DEVICE MANAGER" and hit ENTER.

3. click on your PC.

4. find "UNIVERSAL SERIAL BUS CONTROLLER".

5. find the defective part of your "USB ONTROLLER"
   a hazard logo will show on the part of your "USB CONTROLLER"
  
6. right click it.
7. click "UNINSTALL".(NOTE: it will uninstall the part of your USB port.) 
8. right click the name of your pc at the upper part. and click "SCAN FOR HARDWARE CHANGES"
   (NOTE: it will reinstall the previous part of your USB controller that you have been uninstalled)
